Features
Active

In Weeb Kingdom, we have 24/7 active chats with friendly, welcoming & likeminded members boasting 60K+ messages per day.

Clubs

Outside of the general community chats, we also have clubs. Clubs are mini communities within the community managed by a club leader that are focused around one topic.

Events

Events are organized by our event team weekly. They include things like art contests, Karaoke nights, movie nights, Name That Anime, tournaments and other game-related events such as Jackbox Party Pack.

Fun Bots

The community has our own custom bot based on our official server mascot Warai, including social GIF commands and more. We also have bots such as Myuu, Shoob and Mudae! These bots allow you to give your friends a virtual hug and more!

Great Community

Weeb Kingdom has one of the best and most welcoming communities on Discord with over 35,000+ members. Our server is a safe, inclusive and friendly place to meet new friends with similar interests and hang out.
